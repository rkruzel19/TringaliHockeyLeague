The Tringali Tarpons are back!!!

After being closed for the better part of 2 years, Tringali hockey rink is open and ready to use.

Come out and play

Family Foot Hockey

  • Mon, Wed, Fri @ 6pm
  • Fun for all ages!
  • Only requirement is closed toed shoes and a hockey stick
  • We have extra sticks and protective gear if anybody needs/wants to use it

16+ Roller Hockey pickup

  • Tues & Thurs @ 7pm
  • Pickup style games
  • Experienced and fast paced
  • Uses a puck

Dek Hockey pickup (foot hockey)

  • Sun @ 9am
  • Pickup style games
  • Uses a ball
  • Required: hockey stick
  • Recommended: gloves, shin pads

Reasons To Come Out and Play

  • Plenty of exercise
  • Promotes teamwork and social skills
  • Fun for the whole family
  • Support your local youth sports
  • Spread the word about hockey in Englewood
  • Meet new people

Our main focus will be Family Foot Hockey. Hopefully through this, we'll be able to get kids (16 & under) interested in playing hockey even if they've never played before. While we're still growing, everyone who shows up will be split into 2 teams and we'll have a scrimmage game. As we grow, we hope to be able to get enough kids to have actual teams and a structured league. Help bring hockey back to Englewood!!!
